Welcome to Elena Chong Loo's portfolio Homepage!

I am an undergraduate at the Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, the #1 ranked undergraduate engineering school... that does not have a doctorate program... in the United States. I am currently a third-year senior, majoring in electrical engineering and mathematics, minoring in robotics, and also pursuing a semiconductor certificate.

I am very passionate about learning new things and teaching people what I know. That's why I founded the Rose MakerLab, a sharing, learning, and teaching community where Rose-Hulman students can share and work on projects of their own interest with other students of diverse skills.

In addition, I believe that mixing art, science, engineering, and mathematics, can create very innovative approaches. Over the past few years, I have done several short videos related to science and engineering, which you can check out at my filmmaking youtube channel.
