Building a RepRap 3D Printer


Reprap Prusa Mendel V2 printing a huge aircraft fan model.

[Project Overview]
My first summer at Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, I built a Prusa Mendel V2 RepRap 3D printer after reading an article about 3D Printing from a magazine. I bought all the necessary materials and 3D printed the parts. It took me a week to built the printer and another week to calibrate it perfectly.
